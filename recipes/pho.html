Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of bone broth, rice noodles, and thinly sliced meat (usually beef). It may also be served with bean sprouts, fresh herbs, limes, chiles, and other garnishes.

Ingredients

  • Bone broth
  • Rice noodles
  • thinly-sliced meat
  • Fresh garnishes

Steps

  1. Readying the meat
  2. Gather your beef parts
  3. Cut your meat into pieces
  4. Cook your beef
  5. Simmer a pot of water in beef bones
  6. Replace the beef bones with beef ingredients
  7. Throw charred onions and ginger into the mix
  8. Boil rice noodles
  9. Add vegetables
  10. Top with spices
  11. Serve with bean sprouts and lime wedges
  12. Include sriracha and hoisin Sauce
  13. Freeze excess portions
  14. Customize your recipe in the future
